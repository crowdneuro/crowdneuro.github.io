About Crowdneuro

Counting cells in brain images has been a fundamental method in neuroscience to investigate the functions and structures of the brain. However, cell counting is a painstaking process that can take hours to identify and count cells of interest and is often subjective and irreproducible. With CROWdNeuro (CROWN), we hope to bring together the knowledge of many neuroscientists to curate a free, open-source cell image database that can be used to build cell classification and segmentation models.

With an open-source cell detection dataset and model, we hope to accelerate neuroscience research as well as a lucrative cell imaging database that can be mined for novel discoveries.
